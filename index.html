Lot Linker Search Tool

Lot Links is the City of Dayton program that uses a state provision for Real Estate Acquisition Process, commonly referred to as "REAP," to take title to tax delinquent abandoned properties in the City of Dayton. The City can then sell those properties to qualified buyers.

This tool streamlines your search by showing only those properties that meet the first four eligibility criteria for the Lot Links program.

The property:
  • is in the City of Dayton.
  • is at least 2 years in arrears on taxes.
  • has not had their tax lien sold.
  • is not on a delinquent tax payment plan.

You can search by address, parcel number, or by visual inspection of the map.

To view the address and parcel number of a location on the map, double click on the blue balloon icon.

If the property does not show up in this search tool, then it is not Lot Links eligible at this time.

Jot down the address AND parcel number of each property that interests you - you will need this info to continue the eligibility determination process.

The data for this tool comes directly from the Montgomery County Treasurers Office database, which is updated at the end of every month. The county's data was last queried for LotLinker on .

Found a Property?

After finding a property, there is a list of tasks that should be done:

  • Familiarize yourself with the rules for REAPing a property.
  • Look at the map on the GIS application to determine if the parcel you are requesting covers the property.
    • Montomery County Auditor GIS Application (Requires Silverlight). Things to look out for:
      • Does the parcel cover the expected structures? House, garage, etc.?
      • Are the neighboring lots vacant, and owned by the same individual? If so, you can include them in the same request.
  • Visit the property to ensure that it is abandoned, taking a picture of the house for your application. It is the buyer's responsibility to ensure that a property is actually abandoned.
  • Email lotlinks@daytonohio.gov with the parcel number and address to ensure that no one else has already applied for the property you want.
  • Fill out the application form.
